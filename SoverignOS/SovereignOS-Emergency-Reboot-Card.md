# SovereignOS — Emergency Reboot Card

*Print this. Keep it in your wallet. Tape it to your mirror. This is the entire system compressed to one page.*

---

## WHEN YOU'RE LOST (60 seconds)

You noticed you're lost. **That noticing is the awareness you think you've lost.** You're already back. Now:

1. One breath. Feel your feet on the ground.
2. What's actually happening right now — minus the story?
3. One clear action from here. Just one.

---

## THE TRIPLE-LOOP DEBUGGER (2-5 minutes)

Run this on any problem, any domain, any time:

**Loop 1 — WHAT'S ACTUALLY HAPPENING?**
Separate fact from story. "Revenue dropped 20%" is fact. "I'm failing" is story. What's the raw situation?

**Loop 2 — WHAT PATTERN IS RUNNING?**
What's the habitual response here? What is it protecting you from? Name it without judgment.

**Loop 3 — WHAT'S THE SKILLFUL RESPONSE?**
If you weren't running the pattern, what would you actually do? Do that.

---

## SUBSTRATE FIRST

Nothing works if the hardware is down. Check these before anything else:

- **Body:** Am I sleeping? Eating? Moving? → If no, fix this first.
- **Money:** Can I meet basic obligations this month? → If no, focus here.
- **Nervous system:** Can I sit with discomfort for 90 seconds? → If no, get support.

---

## BODY RESET (when thinking makes it worse)

- Three slow breaths — exhale longer than inhale
- Hand on chest — hold 30 seconds
- Cold water on face — triggers dive reflex
- Walk 5 minutes — don't think, move

---

## DOMAIN QUICK-CUTS

- **Money spiral →** "What's the next concrete financial action? Do that."
- **Relationship conflict →** "What am I protecting? Is it worth it?"
- **Work paralysis →** "Smallest useful thing in 20 minutes. Go."
- **Health neglect →** "What is my body asking for? Do that."
- **Creative block →** "Make something bad. Finish it."
- **Emotional flooding →** "Name it. Let it be here. Don't act on it."

## FIVE PRINCIPLES

1. **Direct Seeing** — what's actually true here?
2. **Non-Grasping** — what am I clinging to?
3. **Engaged Compassion** — am I present or avoiding?
4. **Skillful Effort** — tend conditions, not outcomes
5. **Interdependence** — nothing exists in isolation
